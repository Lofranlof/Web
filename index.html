My story about how I got into lifting

Yeah, basically I was playing The Last of Us: Part 2 and one of the main characters, Abby, had massive guns instead of human arms,

so I thought to myself: "I want to be as jacked and stacked as her". I went to my mom to ask her for a membership in the closest gym that was 20 minutes away on a bycicle.

From that day, I've been going to the gym on a regular basis. That's how it all started.

How to supplement your training for optimal gains:

  1. SLEEP!

    Sleeping is crucial when it comes to recovering and gaining muscles, do not neglect it!

  2. Eating

    You have to eat enough, so that your body will have the material to recover you with.

  3. Training

    Training is when you tell your muscles it is time to grow, recovery is when they actually grow. It's yin and yang of lifting.

    If you wanna grow muscles, you gotta pay attention to both.

    You have to be consistent with your training and have at least some idea about what you are doing

  4. Supplements
